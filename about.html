RPI Creative Writing Club

Comma is the club for writers, poets, and anyone at RPI who wants to create with words.

About Comma

Comma specializes in hosting a comfortable space for people to get together and write creatively. Each meeting has a workshop followed by free writing. We also take trips to Downtown Troy, hold open mics and more.

Comma History

Comma was started in November 2014 by three creative individuals who had a way with words too elegant for the STEM world. After gathering a small group, Comma was born. Once we become an official club in the Spring of 2015, Comma has grown to be a place for creative fun; a space for prose, poetry and any other form of creative writing, and it continues to get better as time goes on.
