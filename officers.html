Grace Goehler The Dragon

President. Secretary. Writing Organizer. Publicity Chair. Long ago, the four officers lived together in harmony. Then, everything changed when the Writing Organizer attacked. Only the Treasurer, master of all four officers, could stop them, but when the budget needed him most, he vanished.

A hundred years passed and my brother and I discovered the new Treasurer, a Sophomore named Grace. And although her writing skills are great, she has a lot to learn before she's ready to save any money.

But I believe Grace can save the budget.
